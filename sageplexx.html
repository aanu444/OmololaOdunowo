STREAMLINING MULTI-INDUSTRY PRODUCTS: A B2C APPROACH TO PRODUCT MANAGEMENT

IN THE BEGINNING:
As a Product Manager fresh out of EntryLevel, Australia , I was able to bring my prior working experiences in marketing, strategy and research towards contributing to products in various stages of its lifecycle

Some of my primary roles had to do with conducting market research, defining product requirements, developing product roadmaps and supporting sales and marketing teams.
MY CONTRIBUTIONS:
  • Studied consumer needs and the competitive landscape and collected data and customer insights to develop product outcomes and make informed product decisions.
  • Defined product requirements by conducting user research and establishing the product’s features, functionality, and user experience based on market research in developing a product resonating with its target audience.
  • Generated a strategy specifying the product’s development schedule and prioritized features and functionality based on market demand, trends and business objectives.
  • Collaborated with cross-functional teams in ensuring products corresponded with client and organziation goals.
  • Guaranteed compliance with the set product roadmap and its specifications.
  • Participated in product testing in assessing how well existing client products performed and identified areas for improvement.
  • Developed product messaging and collateral, providing sales and product marketing teams with training, and engaging with them in building adoption-driving methods.
RESULTS:
  • Launched Metricks v1, a comprehensive affiliate marketing platform, in six months with a user adoption rate of 30%.
  • Partnered on the launch of Lagos Ride v1 and Lagos Grant v2 under the guidance of the senior product manager
