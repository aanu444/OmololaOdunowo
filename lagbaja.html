EXPANDING BEYOND BUSINESS TO PRODUCT: A PRODUCT CULTURE WORTH BUILDING WITH LAGBAJAMOBILE

IN THE BEGINNING:
Lagbaja Mobile is a Domestic Technology company located in Akure, Nigeria. The company first started out as a service related company with no product of its own. I was brought onboard to build the company’s first ever company product, a web application that makes it easy for clients to book render services - the application was also enhanced to have a digital portal by which renders could apply to be a part of our service poll. Doing this was able to propel an increased client to render demand for our services.
Thereafter, I laid out strong strategic plans for the company’s in-house product line which has seen Fixa and GFX begin to come to life. Lagbaja as a solution has been able to further expand its reach in Akure even as plans to reach new heights are underway.
MY CONTRIBUTIONS:
  • Implemented an Agile product culture on the development team and curated high level requirements that got the team working faster and more collaboratively.
  • Pioneered the launch of Lagbaja’s first company product and the ideation and implementation behind the companies product line - Fixa and GFX.
  • Ideated on the second version of Metricks, documented requirements and features expected to strategically position the company along its plans for expansion.
  • Acted as the bridge between product and marketing and spearheaded strategies for launch campaigns.
  • Made effective use of project management tools in keeping track of the team's progress and experimented with Agile frameworks in advocating for quicker deliverables.
RESULTS:
  • The company product earned a feature on Tech Point's 9 innovative startups in Akure to watch in 2023
  • Increased the team's delivery and collaborative process by 71% in 6 months.
  • Expand business strategies beyond the local birthplace of Lagbaja Mobile and moved Lagbaja from an offline service to a stable product in the marketplace
  • Ideated and executed a new product line of efficient Domestic Technology products which should see Lagbaja Mobile expand out of the Akure space and take the national market by storm
  • Observed the competition and secured great market space for the first ever product launch.
