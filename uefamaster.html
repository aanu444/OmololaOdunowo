LAUNCHING MASTERCARD'S SPENDNWIN CAMPAIGN: BRINGING JOY TO NIGERIA'S UEFA FANS

View Campaign Prototype
IN THE BEGINNING:
Every year, MasterCard offers its unwavering support towards the annual UEFA Champions League games. Around April, 2023, I was contacted to build up an impromptu team in delivering a Gaming software for the 2023 Spend and Win MasterCard Campaign that would see one lucky winner visit Istanbul to watch the games with all expenses paid just like they did in the year 2022. I was able to curate the team and get experienced hands to hop on the project and we were able to successfully execute the design, development and functional delivery of a mobile responsive web application, alongside an admin and super admin dashboard interface which recorded an average user base of about 4,000 users per every activation day totalling a sum of N45,000+ users for the duration of its 6 week activation campaign period. With the team, I was able to quickly report and resolve downtimes and worked round the clock 24/7 and oftentimes at the venue in person to ensure issues were resolved in a timely fashion.
MY CONTRIBUTIONS:
  • Brought together and led a product development team that was able to execute and deliver 3 applications in the space of 1 month.
  • Handled stakeholder crisis ranging from on-field marketing concerns to user experience blockers.
  • Ensured minimal bugs were reported across the many locations the campaign was set up in.
  • Implemented business level strategies that fostered an increased adoption to conversion and revenue generation rate across platform product; Elva Learn Academy
  • Built an ease of access admin and super admin panel that enabled access controls and location access.
  • Effectively managed about 45,000+ users experience with the application within the 6 week activation period
RESULTS:
  • Recorded massive user turnout and an overall great user experience with little to no technical glitches per activation day.
  • Had a successful MasterCard x UEFA campaign.
  • Delivered a relatable UI to football fans and upgraded difficulty levels at the request of relevant stakeholders
