LAUNCHING MASTERCARD'S SPENDNWIN CAMPAIGN: BRINGING JOY TO NIGERIA'S UEFA FANS

IN THE BEGINNING:
Every year, MasterCard offers its unwavering support towards the annual UEFA Champions League games. Around April, 2023, I was contracted to build up an impromptu team in delivering a Gaming software for the 2023 Spend and Win MasterCard Campaign that would see one lucky winner visit Istanbul to watch the game's Final match.

Gathering professionals in my close network was the first step. Next, the team and I dedicated 15 hours out of 24 hours in the day to planning, designing and developing 3 interface applications - the gaming application itself, an admin and super-admin platform which handled high volumes of user interaction per activation day.
MY CONTRIBUTIONS:
  • Identified and prioritized features, enhancements, and bug fixes that will have the most significant impact on customer satisfaction and business goals.
  • By leveraging data analytics and user behavior metrics, I was able to measure the impact of product changes in minizing risk thereby increasing the likelihood of product success.
  • Established the scope, objectives, deliverables, and timelines of the project, and made use of effective tools and methods to collaborate, share, and track progress.
RESULTS:
  • Efficiently oversaw the experience of over 45,000 users with the application during the six-week activation period.
  • Access controls and location access were enabled through the construction of an easy-to-use admin and super admin panel.
  • Delivered a relatable UI to football fans and upgraded difficulty levels at the request of relevant stakeholders
