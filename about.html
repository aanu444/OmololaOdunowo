ABOUT ME
As a Product Manager and Owner, my approach to Product Management is to guarantee the product actually solves a user problem. However, my focus is in ensuring that the right solutions create value for companies. My super power lies in my ability to combine intuition and creativity with data in making think-fast decisions.

I enjoy collaborating with development teams and other stakeholders in building, marketing and selling products and features while viewing the future of the product through a long term strategic and tactical backed lens.

  • User Focused: I always keep the focus on users and ensure product to feature releases as well as strategies that benefit the business never make users feel left out

  • Strong Product Cultures: I focus on building strong, cross-functional and empowered product teams

  • Strategic Alignment of Business and Product: I plan and implement practices that ensure company strategies support its general objectives

  • Creative and Innovative Thinking: I never confine myself to one way of thinking. I enjoy thinking outside of the box and looking into uncharted waters

  • Agile Framework and Design Sprints: Through all stages of the products lifecycle, I am able to assess and prioritize what needs to be done quickly and efficiently

  • Analytical Sense and Proactive Strategies: I enjoy observing user behaviour and making informed data driven decisions in curating proactive solutions to problems before they blow up
